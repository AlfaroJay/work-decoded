For over 20 years, I worked in HR and operations management—the person sitting across the table making hiring decisions, managing performance issues, and navigating workplace politics from the inside. I've seen how the game is played, and I know what HR is really thinking.

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I get an appointment?

Most clients can book within 24-48 hours. For urgent situations, we do our best to accommodate same-day or next-day appointments.

What if I'm not sure which service I need?

No worries! Complete our intake form and describe your situation. We'll match you with the right consultant and service level.

Is my consultation confidential?

Absolutely. All consultations are completely confidential. We will never share your information with your employer or any third party.

Do you provide legal advice?

Work Decoded provides workplace advocacy and career guidance. We are not attorneys and do not provide legal advice. If your situation involves potential legal issues, we'll let you know and recommend you consult with an employment attorney.

What if my situation is really urgent?

Select "urgent" on your intake form and we'll prioritize your assignment and booking.

Who will I be working with?

All Work Decoded consultants are certified HR professionals (PHR/SHRM-CP or SPHR/SHRM-SCP) with 5-10+ years of experience. You'll be matched based on your specific situation and needs, and you can view consultant profiles and select your preferred consultant when booking.

What if I need more than one session?

Many clients benefit from ongoing support, especially through complex situations. Our session packages offer 10-15% savings for multiple sessions, and you can work with the same consultant throughout.

Do you offer refunds?

Single sessions are non-refundable once completed. Packages are non-refundable, but unused sessions may be transferred. If we determine we cannot help with your situation after reviewing your intake form, we will issue a full refund before the session takes place.
